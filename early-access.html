What is AnyPhone?

The phone system for modern small businesses

AnyPhone helps small businesses handle calls and messages without giving every customer a personal cell number or buying an enterprise phone system. Get a local or toll-free number, set up menus and extensions, route calls, manage shared texts and voicemails, receive voicemail transcripts, and keep your team updated in Slack.

As your business grows, AnyPhone is designed to support additional numbers, channels like WhatsApp, and third-party AI services.

Early access questions

When is AnyPhone launching?
AnyPhone is launching soon. Get early access and we’ll send updates as setup becomes available.
Can I get a number today?
Not yet. Early access lets you join the list before public launch. When AnyPhone is ready, you’ll be able to get a local or toll-free number or bring your current number.
Can I bring my current business number?
Yes, AnyPhone is designed to support number porting. We’ll share more details when early access opens.
Is AnyPhone only for one number?
No. You can start with a local or toll-free number and add numbers as needed.
Is AnyPhone an AI receptionist?
No. AnyPhone is a phone system for modern small businesses. It is designed to connect with third-party AI services when your business needs them, but AnyPhone itself is not positioned as an AI receptionist.
Who is AnyPhone for?
AnyPhone is for modern small businesses that need a real phone system without enterprise complexity. It is built for founders, owners, small teams, contractors, assistants, and businesses that use tools like Slack.
