Questions before you start

How fast can I be up and running?
Minutes. Pick a number, add your details, and your line is live — no hardware to ship, no technician, no waiting.
Can I keep my current number?
Yes. Choose “Keep mine” above and we'll email you simple steps to port it over for free after you sign up. Your number keeps working the whole time.
Is there a contract?
No. Every plan is month-to-month — upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime from your dashboard.
Do I need to buy any equipment?
No. JOEL runs on the phone and computer you already have. A VoIP desk phone is optional if you want a handset.
When am I charged?
Your first month is billed when you activate. After that it's the same date each month, and you can cancel anytime before your next bill.
Can I change my plan later?
Anytime. Start on Grow and move up to Max when you want advanced integrations and custom workflows, or down to Lite — changes take effect on your next bill.
