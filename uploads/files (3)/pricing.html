Pricing questions

Can I change plans later?

Anytime. Upgrade or downgrade in a click — changes take effect on your next bill.

What happens if I go over my minutes?

Calls keep working. Extra minutes are billed at a low per-minute rate, or just move up a plan.

Is there a contract?

No. Every plan is month-to-month and you can cancel whenever you want.

Do I need to buy hardware?

No. JOEL works on the phones and computers you already have. A VoIP desk phone is optional.

Can I keep my current number?

Yes — moving your existing number over is free on every plan.

Does it work with Slack and other apps?

Yes. On Max, JOEL sends call and text notifications to Slack and WhatsApp, and connects to your CRM, Zapier, and thousands of other apps you already use.
