About JOEL

Small businesses are entering their most powerful era in history.

Founders can now combine employees, contractors, software, automation, AI, and services to build businesses that would have required much larger teams just a decade ago. Yet most software is still built around the assumptions of larger organizations.

We built JOEL for the way modern small businesses actually operate.

The team

Built by the team behind companies serving hundreds of thousands of small businesses.

The team behind JOEL has founded, built, and invested in companies across software, healthcare, consumer products, and services. Together, those businesses have served hundreds of thousands of small businesses over the last two decades. JOEL is built on the lessons learned from working alongside founders through every stage of growth.
