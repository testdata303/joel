What JOEL is

A real business phone system for small teams.

JOEL is the phone system between your customers and the people who can help them. Customers call or text your business number. JOEL routes calls by function, keeps texts with the business, sends voicemail transcripts, and notifies your team when something needs attention.

Why Slack matters

Your team already works in Slack. Your phone system should not hide somewhere else.

Small teams use Slack to coordinate work, but calls, texts, and voicemails usually live in a separate phone app. That means someone has to remember to check the dashboard, check the voicemail, check the inbox, and tell the team what happened. JOEL brings the important phone events into Slack.

Questions

Questions about JOEL and Slack

What is JOEL?
JOEL is a simple phone system for small businesses. It gives you one business number, a simple menu, call routing, a shared text inbox, voicemail with transcripts, and Slack notifications for the events your team should not miss.
Is JOEL a phone system or a Slack app?
JOEL is the phone system. Slack is where JOEL can send notifications for missed calls, new texts, and voicemails.
Can JOEL send missed calls to Slack?
Yes. When a call comes in and nobody answers, JOEL can post a missed call notification to the Slack channel you choose so the team can follow up.
Can JOEL send voicemail transcripts to Slack?
Yes. When a voicemail comes in, JOEL can send a notification with the transcript so the team knows what the caller needed.
Can JOEL send new text notifications to Slack?
Yes. When a customer texts your business number, JOEL can notify the right Slack channel so the message does not sit unseen.
Can I reply to texts from Slack?
Not in this version. Slack notifications help your team see what happened. Replies and follow-up happen in JOEL.
Can I answer phone calls in Slack?
No. JOEL routes calls to phones, lines, assistants, contractors, or call centers. Slack is for notifications.
