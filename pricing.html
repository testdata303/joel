FAQ

Questions, answered.

Do you charge per user?
No. Users are unlimited on every plan. Plan limits apply to numbers, extensions, outbound minutes, and advanced capabilities.
Are extensions unlimited?
Lite includes 2 extensions. Grow includes 10. Max includes unlimited extensions.
What counts toward my minutes?
Only outbound minutes count. Inbound calls do not use your included minutes.
What happens if I exceed my minutes?
AnyPhone adds a 1,000-minute pack for $10. You will see the charge clearly on your bill.
Can I keep my current number?
Yes. Porting is free, and your number keeps working while the transfer completes.
Can I text from Lite?
Yes, after the required SMS compliance approval.
Do I need hardware?
No. AnyPhone works on the mobile phone and computer you already have. Compatible SIP desk phones are optional on Grow and Max.
Is support included?
Yes. Human support is included with every plan.
