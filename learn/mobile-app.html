The problem

Calling customers back should not expose your personal number.

Small business calls do not only happen from an office. Owners call from the road. Assistants call from home. Contractors or remote team members may need to return customer calls. Without a business calling app, those calls often come from personal cell numbers. That creates confusion for customers and makes the business harder to manage.

How it fits

The app is one part of your phone system.

AnyPhone is more than a calling app. It is a phone system for modern small businesses. Get a local or toll-free number, add numbers as needed, set up menus and extensions, route calls, manage shared texts and voicemails, receive voicemail transcripts, and keep your team updated in Slack.

The mobile app handles one important job: making outgoing calls from your business number when you are not sitting at a desk.

Questions

Questions about the mobile app

What is the AnyPhone mobile app for?
The AnyPhone mobile app lets you make outgoing business calls from your phone using your AnyPhone business number as the caller ID.
Will customers see my personal cell number?
No. When you make calls through the AnyPhone mobile app, customers see your business number instead of your personal cell number.

No. Incoming calls can route to the phones, lines, assistants, contractors, or call centers you choose.
Can team members make calls from the business number?
Yes, the mobile app is designed so owners, assistants, contractors, or remote team members can make business calls from the right number.
Is AnyPhone just a mobile calling app?
No. AnyPhone is a phone system for modern small businesses. The mobile app is for outgoing calls from your business number.

Yes. AnyPhone is designed to support local and toll-free numbers, with the ability to add numbers as needed.
