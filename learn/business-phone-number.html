What it is

A number that belongs to the company, not one person.

A business phone number is the number customers call and text to reach your company. With AnyPhone it is more than a line: it carries a menu and extensions, routes to the right person, keeps texts in a shared inbox, and turns voicemail into transcripts, so communication belongs to the business.

Why it matters

A real business number builds trust and keeps cells private.

Customers trust a business number more than a personal cell, and a business number keeps your private line out of circulation. More important, it lets the whole team share the work: calls route to the right person, texts land in a shared inbox, and voicemail becomes a transcript. The number, and the customer relationship, belong to the business.

What is a business phone number?
It is the number customers call and text to reach your company. With AnyPhone it also carries routing, extensions, shared texts, and voicemail transcripts, so it belongs to the business, not one person.
Can I get a local or toll-free number?
Yes. Choose a local number in the area code you want or a toll-free number, and use both together if it helps. See local and toll-free numbers.

Yes. AnyPhone is designed to support number porting. Your number keeps working while the transfer completes.

Yes. Start with one number and add numbers as needed, for a second location, a department, or a campaign.

Yes. Calls route to the right person, texts land in a shared inbox, and voicemail becomes a transcript, so communication stays with the business.
Can I text from my business number?
Yes. Customers can text your business number, and the conversation lands in a shared inbox for the team to answer.
