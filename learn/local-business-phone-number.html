Questions

Questions about local business numbers

Can I choose my area code?
Yes. Pick a local number in the city and area code your customers know.
Can I keep my current local number?
Yes. AnyPhone is designed to support number porting. Your number keeps working while the transfer completes.
Can I have a local and a toll-free number?
Yes. Use a local number, add a toll-free number, and add numbers as needed.
Does a local number help my SEO?
We do not claim it does. A local number is about being reachable and familiar to nearby customers, not about search rankings.
Does it keep my personal cell private?
Yes. Customers see your local business number, and you can forward calls to your cell so your personal number stays private.
Is it just a number, or a phone system?
Both. Your local number comes with routing, extensions, shared texts, and voicemail transcripts. See virtual phone system.
