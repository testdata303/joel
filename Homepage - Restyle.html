WHY FlyLine

A complete phone system without per-user pricing.

Google Voice works well for basic calling inside Google Workspace. Grasshopper keeps forwarding simple. Quo, Dialpad, and Aircall are capable products built around paid users. FlyLine gives the whole company one flexible phone system.

Good for basic calling inside Google Workspace. Team access and more advanced phone-system features are tied to Google users and licenses.
Grasshopper
Simple forwarding and extensions, but built before Slack, WhatsApp, shared messaging, and bring-your-own AI became normal.
Per-user phone systems
Quo, Dialpad, and Aircall are capable, but the bill grows whenever another teammate, assistant, or contractor needs access.
