ExampleA missed emergency call shows up in Slack and the on-call plumber calls back fast.

Questions

Questions plumbers ask

Can I use my existing plumbing business number?
Yes. You can port the number your customers already call into JOEL and keep it as your business number. If you would rather start fresh, you can get a new number for your area instead.
Can calls ring my cell phone?
Yes. You can forward calls to your cell, the office line, an assistant, a dispatcher, or a call center. Your personal number stays private because customers only ever see the business number.
Can I send and receive texts from the business number?
Yes. Texts to the business number land in one shared inbox. The team can read and reply from the same place, so a customer message never gets stuck on one person's phone.
Can different types of calls go to different people?
Yes. Set up a simple menu and route each option however you like. New jobs can ring the owner and office, emergencies can ring the owner and a backup plumber, and existing customers can ring the dispatcher.
What happens if nobody answers?
The call goes to voicemail. You get a transcript and an email, and the missed call and voicemail post to Slack so someone on the team can call the customer back.
Is JOEL dispatch software?
No. JOEL is your phone system. It helps calls and texts reach the right person. It does not schedule jobs, assign technicians, or manage invoices.
