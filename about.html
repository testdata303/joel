Core values
Our F.A.S.T. core values
F. Founders
For customers

We build for founders. The smallest company gets the same respect as the largest. We give them control, flexibility, and free human-based support so they can grow without being forced to add unnecessary cost or complexity.

For our team

We act like owners. We use first principles, leverage tools and people to do more with less, and remove everything that does not create real value.

A. Agency
For customers

The customer stays in control. They choose their tools, people, contractors, and AI. We adapt to them. We do not lock them in.

For our team

We own the outcome. We make decisions with the information we have, solve problems end to end, and move fast without waiting for permission when action is reversible.

S. Simplicity
For customers
