Questions before you start

How fast can I be up and running?
Minutes. Choose a number, add your details, and your line is live. No hardware, technician, or waiting period is required.
Can I keep my current number?
Yes. Choose “Keep mine,” complete signup, and we will guide you through the free transfer. Your number keeps working while the port completes.
Am I locked into a long contract?
No. Every plan is month-to-month. Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel from your dashboard.
Do you charge per user?
No. Users are unlimited. Add teammates, assistants, contractors, and occasional users without another seat fee.
Do I need to buy equipment?
No. AnyPhone works on the mobile phone and computer you already have. A compatible SIP desk phone is optional on Grow and Max.
Can I connect Slack, WhatsApp, or an AI answering service?
Yes. Depending on your plan, AnyPhone can send notifications to Slack, add WhatsApp to your business number, trigger webhooks, and route an extension to the AI answering service you choose.
When am I charged?
Your first month is charged when you activate. After that, you are billed on the same date each month.
Can I change my plan later?
Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect on your next bill.
