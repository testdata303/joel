Questions plumbers ask us

Can emergency calls reach me after hours?
Yes. Set your own emergency and after-hours rules — urgent calls ring your cell (or the on-call tech) around the clock, while everything else can take a message. The 2 a.m. burst pipe gets to you; the spam doesn't.
Can I send quotes, invoices, and take payment?
Yes. Text estimates, deposit links, and final invoices right from your business number, so customers can pay by text — often before you've left the job. Everything lives in one thread per customer.
Can calls ring more than one tech?
Absolutely. Ring your whole crew at once and the first to pick up takes the job, or set an on-call rotation so the right person answers. No call slips through.
Can I keep the number my customers already call?
Yes — porting your existing number is free, and it keeps working the whole time we move it over. Or grab a fresh local number in your service area.
Do I need any equipment or a contract?
Neither. JOEL runs on the phone you already carry — no desk phone, nothing to ship — and every plan is month-to-month. Cancel anytime, no fees.
